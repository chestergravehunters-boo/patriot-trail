The Chesterland Patriot Trail is an America 250 project created by the Chester Grave Hunters to honor the Revolutionary War patriots whose journeys ended in Chesterland, Ohio. This project connects our community to the earliest chapters of the nation’s story and preserves the lives of those remembered here.

The trail includes eleven stops across Chesterland’s three historic cemeteries. Each marker includes a QR code that opens the story of the patriot or widow remembered at that site. Wander, scan, and discover the lives that helped shape our community.
